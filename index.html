Biography

Email: yjiaweneecs at gmail dot com

I am a senior research scientist at PAII Inc. Before joining PAII in 2019, I obtained my PhD degree in Computer Science from University of Texas at Arlington under supervision from Dr. Junzhou Huang. I hold both Master’s and Bachelor’s Degree from Xi’an Jiaotong University in China. My research interests include computer vision and machine learning with a focus on medical imaging applications with deep learning-based approaches.

I worked as research intern at NVIDIA and PAII in 2018 Summer and 2018 Fall, respectively.
